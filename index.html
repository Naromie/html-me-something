A Winter In OTTOWA

I have been in Cananada in many different season Summer;Fall;Sprint; Winter

My Last trip was last Winter and this was the one that really stuck with me

I Did not enjoy it.

I have spent 21 Days there and I have never been outside. It kept snowing all day long. A day that a people from New York would tag as a storm or a blizzard is like a regular snowing day for Canadians.
